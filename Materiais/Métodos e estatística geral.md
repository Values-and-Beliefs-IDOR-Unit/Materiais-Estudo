# Métodos e estatística geral

> “Statistics is complicated, and so is science. There is no singular right way to do either, and universally acceptable compromises may not exist. Statistics offers a wide array of tools for assisting with scientific inference by calibrating uncertainty, but statistical inference is not a substitute for scientific inference.” [Lew, 2020](https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/164_2019_286#notes)
